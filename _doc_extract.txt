The Organization
PRD Consultants is an international development consulting and advisory firm operating through registered entities in the United Kingdom and Pakistan. PRD Consultants Ltd is registered with Companies House under the laws of England and Wales, while PRD Consultants (Private) Limited is incorporated under the corporate laws of Pakistan and registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP). Originally established under the Companies Ordinance, 1984, the company is currently governed by the Companies Act, 2017.
Building on more than a decade of development and humanitarian experience through Partners for Research and Development (PRD), PRD Consultants combines practical field knowledge with professional consulting expertise to support governments, development partners, donor-funded programmes, international organizations, NGOs, research institutions, and private sector organizations.
Through its United Kingdom Head Office and Pakistan operations, PRD Consultants provides consulting, research, technical assistance, project management, monitoring, evaluation, accountability and learning (MEAL), institutional strengthening, and strategic advisory services across a broad range of development sectors.
Supported by a multidisciplinary network of national and international experts, the firm delivers evidence-based, innovative, and context-sensitive solutions that help organizations strengthen institutions, improve programme performance, enhance accountability, and achieve sustainable development outcomes.

About Us
From Development Practice to Development Consulting
PRD Consultants was established on a simple belief: effective development solutions are built on experience, evidence, innovation, and strong partnerships.
Our journey began in 2010 through Partners for Research and Development (PRD), an organization dedicated to humanitarian assistance, community development, public health, research, training, and social development initiatives. Through direct engagement with communities, government institutions, development partners, and civil society organizations, PRD gained valuable insights into the realities of development implementation and the factors that contribute to sustainable impact.
During the floods of 2010 and 2011 in Pakistan, PRD worked alongside communities and stakeholders to support relief, recovery, and resilience-building efforts. These experiences strengthened our understanding of programme delivery, community engagement, institutional systems, and evidence-based development practice.
As development programmes became increasingly complex, results-driven, and data-oriented, the need for high-quality consulting, technical assistance, monitoring and evaluation, and strategic advisory services continued to grow. In response, PRD Consultants was established as a professional development consulting and advisory firm serving governments, development partners, donor-funded programmes, international organizations, NGOs, research institutions, and private sector organizations.
Today, PRD Consultants combines practical field experience with professional consulting expertise. Through our United Kingdom headquarters and Pakistan operations, we provide innovative, evidence-based, and context-sensitive solutions that help clients strengthen institutions, improve programme performance, enhance accountability, and achieve sustainable development outcomes.
Our unique foundation enables us to bridge the gap between strategy and implementation, transforming evidence into action and development investments into lasting impact.

Humanitarian and Community Development Experience
PRD Consultants’ foundation is built upon years of direct engagement with communities affected by emergencies, vulnerabilities, and development challenges. Through Partners for Research and Development (PRD), the organization gained practical experience in humanitarian response, community development, public health, research, and social development initiatives.
During the devastating floods of 2010 and 2011 in Sindh, PRD implemented humanitarian response and early recovery initiatives to support affected and vulnerable communities. These interventions provided valuable experience in emergency coordination, community engagement, service delivery, and recovery planning.
Key areas of engagement included:
Emergency relief assistance for flood-affected communities.
Distribution of food and non-food assistance to displaced and vulnerable populations.
Support for safe drinking water, basic humanitarian needs, and community services.
Community-based health promotion and awareness initiatives.
Maternal and child health and nutrition awareness activities.
Community mobilization and volunteer-led support mechanisms.
Coordination with local institutions, government stakeholders, and humanitarian partners.
These experiences developed PRD’s strong understanding of humanitarian principles, community participation, stakeholder coordination, and sustainable recovery approaches.
